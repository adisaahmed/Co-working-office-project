little description of Colony location

Details: Located on the third floor of a building with character at the heart of the commercial district in Ikeja, Colony Allen Avenue has a comfortable vibe with a mix of open areas, offices and quiet nooks. This is the original Colony location and hosts many events and activities, so you won't have to go far to connect with fellow members and the Ikeja startup community.


Amenities: Fast and secure wifi, Printer, scanner, and fax machine, Free office supplies, Meeting rooms with full A/V capabilities, Phone booths, Bottomless coffee, still and sparkling water, fruit and snacks, Variety of standing, sitting, and soft seating workspaces, community kitchen space.


Membership Options: Make Colony Allen Avenue your main base or periodic workplace. Combine space & membership options to get what you need. We seek a vibrant mix of skills & industries to lead to all kinds of new connections and collaborations.


Hours: We are open for all members and guests Monday through Friday from 9 AM to 6.00 PM, except major holidays. Office memberships always have 24/7 key card access.


Direction and Parking: Colony Allen Avenue is at 48 Allen Avenue directly opposite Union Bank branch office and three (3) buildings after the KFC restaurant. Parking is on the street directly behind the building, so visitors can take the turning right immediately after the KFC restaurant and park for a fee in front of the local government office.
